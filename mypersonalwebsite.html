ATHOBIOGRAPHY

My story

My name is Chiara Fay J. Sanchez. I was born on June 17th, 2006 in a rural area of bohol, where my mom's hometown and her family resides. My parents were Nena J. Sanchez and Jerson L. Sanchez. Both my parents are working hard to provide what we need. My mother is a housewife with a small business and my father is a technician. I am fortunate to have parents like them that support us in every way.

Since I was a child my childhood was quite different to others. When I was young, because of my parents' work we constantly moved to another city. Changing schools is hard for me because, as a child, I missed my friends and schoolmates. But with my two sisters I can carry on with it.

In my elementary days I changed schools 5 times but now in high school I study at Caraga State University where I currently went to school there. A lot happened in my highschool life. As the philosopher says "It was only highschool after all, definitely one of the most bizarre periods in a person's life"- EA, BUCCHIANERI. I grow, I learn new things, discover new emotions, have precious memories and other more. A treasure indeed. A treasure that can be kept and bejeweled in memories.

Writing this short autobiography brings back a lot of memories that I forgot. Even though life has many ups and downs, challenges, and failures, we did not stop because we have a mechanism called 'fighting'. Fighting the battle through life is what makes us a warrior and be the victor of this war. As I continue writing my story. I hope that my future self remembers all the hardship, sadness, failures, happiness, achievement in every chapter in my story.
